# Channel Plan — Anna Brand Channel

## Goal (one sentence)

**Own the term "agentic parenting" and build a 10K-follower mom audience in 6 months that compounds into Anna signups.**

---

## The 10-line plan

- Single brand channel, two voices: real Dilani (~60%) + Anna-as-character (~40%), all AI content labeled
- Core format: "Dilani throws to Anna-as-agent" (real-Dilani + Remotion UI demo, not AI avatar) — algorithm-safe, product-clear
- 5 posts/week: 2 agent-demo, 1 avatar explainer (TikTok-only), 1 reaction/stitch, 1 pure Dilani
- Distribution: IG + FB Reels (cross-post), TikTok, YouTube Shorts (13s + 60s cuts) — 4 platforms, ~zero extra effort
- Supporting assets: weekly newsletter (Beehiiv), one named recurring format, Pinterest quote-cards per video
- Pillar SEO/GEO: `/blog/guides/what-is-agentic-parenting` live; ship 2-3 supporting posts over 2 weeks
- Leading metrics (weekly): GEO citations (ChatGPT/Perplexity), save rate >2%, branded search trend, referral visits to hianna.ai
- Milestones: 1K followers @ 8-12 weeks, 10K @ 6-9 months, 100K @ 18-24 months
- Soft product-plug only after 6-8 weeks + 2-5K followers; never before
- Kill-switches: chat-with-Anna site, stable of 10 channels, LinkedIn, HeyGen-Dilani-avatar, Discord

## The long version

### Why this channel and why now

The term "agentic parenting" started circulating publicly in April 2026 (a16z podcast + Claire Vo threads on X). The blog pillar at `hianna.ai/blog/guides/what-is-agentic-parenting` went live on day one of the term existing, which gives us first-mover position on an uncontested keyword. Content turns that blog authority into social discovery, and vice versa. A short-form channel that consistently explains and demonstrates agentic parenting (with Anna as the lived example) is the single most leveraged thing Anna can do for top-of-funnel right now.

### Why single-channel, not a stable

We considered running 3-10 channels in parallel (Dilani, Mario-as-dad, AI-Anna standalone, Pixar shorts, WTF-parenting, HeyGen-Dilani-avatar, user-generated channels). Research on platform treatment of AI content in 2026 — see `docs/SCOPE.md` evidence section — showed:

- Meta platforms (IG + FB Reels) actively downrank AI-flagged content
- YouTube has mass-suspended thousands of faceless AI channels
- TikTok excludes AI-dominated content from monetization
- Face-to-camera real human content consistently outperforms AI-only on all platforms
- Multiple channels split founder attention and underperform a single-cadence channel

The single-channel decision preserves algorithmic trust, concentrates growth, and defers parallelism to when we have a proven format to replicate (past 10K followers).

### The two voices

**Dilani (real, founder).** ~60% of posts. iPhone front camera, her actual kitchen, no studio. Provides the authenticity anchor the algorithms reward. Builds founder personal brand simultaneously.

**Anna (character).** ~40% of posts. Explicitly labeled AI. Maya-avatar for talking-head explainers (TikTok primary, which tolerates AI best); Anna-as-agent UI demos (Remotion-rendered chat screens) for product-showing content that runs on all platforms without triggering AI detection.

### The four content types (per week)

1. **Dilani throws to Anna-as-agent (2 per week).** Real-Dilani speaks to camera, cuts to Anna-agent UI demo showing the product doing the thing, cuts back. ~80% of frame time is real-Dilani which keeps Meta algorithms happy. The Remotion UI demo reads as "software", not "AI synthetic human", so no detection penalty.
2. **Anna-avatar explainer (1 per week, TikTok primary).** Maya (photorealistic AI character) talks to camera about an agentic parenting concept. Clearly labeled AI. TikTok tolerates this; cross-post to Shorts; skip IG/FB Reels for this format.
3. **Dilani reacting to trending content (1 per week).** Stitch or green-screen format. Reacts to AI-agent demos, working-mom content, productivity-influencer videos, other founders' AI demos. Rides borrowed virality. Founder-led = zero AI-detection risk.
4. **Pure Dilani face-to-camera (1 per week).** No AI anywhere. Teaching moment, behind-the-scenes, personal story. The "insurance" post every week that keeps the account's real-human ratio high.

### Distribution

One source, four destinations:

| Platform | Content types posted | How |
|----------|---------------------|-----|
| TikTok | All 4 types | Primary post target |
| Instagram Reels | Types 1, 3, 4 (skip Anna-avatar) | Manual post OR cross-post tool |
| Facebook Reels | Same as Instagram | Meta's one-toggle cross-post from IG |
| YouTube Shorts | All 4 types, cut to 13s hook version + full version | Dual-upload per post |

Bonus for YouTube specifically: every 4 weeks, combine the month's Shorts into one 3-5 min long-form video. Triggers YouTube's 41% compounding-growth effect for Shorts + long-form channels.

### Supporting assets (Tier 1)

1. **Newsletter.** Beehiiv weekly. "The three most useful agentic parenting moves I saw this week." Bio-link on all socials points here.
2. **Named recurring format.** "Anna Explains" or "The Mental Load Memo" — pick one, commit 30 episodes. Builds a cue in followers' heads.
3. **Pinterest quote cards.** 20 min per video → 3-slide carousel for Pinterest. Long-tail search juice specifically in the mom demo.

### Metrics

Leading indicators, checked weekly (not SEO ranking):

- **GEO citations:** manually search "agentic parenting" in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Overview. Do they cite hianna.ai? Log yes/no.
- **Social:** weekly views, avg view duration, save rate (>2% is the health threshold).
- **Branded search volume:** Google Trends on "anna family assistant", "hianna", etc.
- **Referral traffic:** PostHog counts from TikTok/Instagram/YouTube/Facebook to hianna.ai.

### Realistic growth timeline

| Milestone | Realistic | Stretch |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 1,000 followers | 8-12 weeks | 4-6 weeks if one post hits |
| 10,000 followers | 6-9 months | 3-4 months with a viral |
| 100,000 followers | 18-24 months | 12 months with repeat virals + cross-platform compound |

90% of accounts trying this never hit 10K. The ones that do post 3-5x/week for 6+ months straight. Cadence > quality-per-post.

### When to add a second channel

Not now. At 10K on the main channel, with a proven format and hook library, adding Mario-as-dad or user-generated channels becomes genuinely leveraged. Before that, splitting attention is the #1 failure mode.

### Kill-switches (things we're explicitly not doing)

- Chat-with-Anna / chat-with-Dilani website — parents don't want this.
- Stable of 10 channels — platforms actively punish this pattern in 2026.
- LinkedIn channel — tempting for Dilani's founder voice but wrong audience right now. Revisit in 3-6 months.
- HeyGen avatar of Dilani — deepfake-of-real-individual category, 0-5% reach, EU AI Act fine risk.
- Discord / Slack community — moderation overhead kills it at this scale. Revisit at 10K newsletter subs.
- Multiple simultaneous named formats — dilutes the "it's the Anna Explains one" recognition.
